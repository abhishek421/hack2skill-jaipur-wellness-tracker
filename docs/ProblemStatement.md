Challenge:

Mental Wellness Tracker

Build a solution that helps students monitor and improve their mental well-being during board exams, competitive entrance tests, and result seasons.

Students preparing for exams such as NEET, JEE, CUET, CAT, GATE, UPSC, and various board examinations often face stress, anxiety, burnout, self-doubt, and uncertainty.

Create a simple and engaging tool that helps students:

Track their mood
Identify stress triggers
Reflect on their emotions
Receive personalized wellness support throughout their academic journey
